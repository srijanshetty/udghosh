From the days of ancient Olympics, the one thing that has brought people closer, one thing that has inculcated valor, leadership, courage and skill, one thing that has repeatedly emphasized the importance of teamwork and harmony, it is sports.

UDGHOSH, Annual Sports Festival of IIT Kanpur, is built on the same foundations and seeks to propagate the spirit of sports. It is an arena for student athletes from across the country to lock horns in an epic battle, each determined by the last man standing. Every year, It sees hordes of academicians and students transform into raving sports fanatics. With the superb infrastructure and cut-tough competition you can be sure that you don't want to miss this one.
